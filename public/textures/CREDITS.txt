TEXTURE CREDITS & ATTRIBUTION
=============================
solar-system app (v1.8) — public/textures/

All shipped textures are redistributable (CC-BY 4.0 or public domain).
No PlanetPixelEmporium or paid/commercial-pack assets are used.


1. Solar System Scope — https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/
   License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC-BY 4.0)
   https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/
   "Textures created by Solar System Scope, distributed under CC-BY 4.0."

   Files acquired/transcoded from this source in this commit:
     2k_earth_nightmap.jpg   (Earth night lights / Black Marble style)
     2k_earth_normal.jpg     (Earth normal map; transcoded from SSS 2k_earth_normal_map.tif)
     2k_earth_specular.jpg   (Earth specular / ocean mask; transcoded from SSS 2k_earth_specular_map.tif)

   Files already in the repo that also originate from this source:
     2k_earth_daymap.jpg, 2k_earth_clouds.jpg,
     2k_saturn.jpg, 2k_saturn_ring_alpha.png,
     moons/2k_luna.jpg (= SSS 2k_moon.jpg),
     and the other planet/moon color maps.

   Higher-res (8K) masters for the above are recorded as "acquire" URLs in
   textures.manifest.json for the future local/native build.


2. USGS Astrogeology Science Center / NASA LRO LOLA — public domain
   Product: Moon LRO LOLA Global LDEM 118m (Mar 2014)
   Catalog: https://astrogeology.usgs.gov/search/map/moon_lro_lola_dem_118m
   GeoTIFF: https://planetarymaps.usgs.gov/mosaic/Lunar_LRO_LOLA_Global_LDEM_118m_Mar2014.tif
   Created by the LOLA Science Team, NASA Goddard Space Flight Center;
   published by USGS Astrogeology Science Center.
   Use constraint: public domain — "Please cite authors."

   NOT shipped (8 GB master). Recorded as an "acquire" entry under
   moon.displacement in textures.manifest.json. Bake offline to a
   tangent-space normal or low-res displacement map before use.


3. NASA / NASA Goddard Space Flight Center SVS — public domain
   https://svs.gsfc.nasa.gov/  |  https://visibleearth.nasa.gov/
   Listed as an acceptable public-domain fallback source. No files were
   pulled directly from NASA in this commit (SSS night-lights texture is
   itself derived from NASA Black Marble data and is used under CC-BY 4.0).
